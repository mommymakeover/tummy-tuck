How much Bellafill will be needed to treat my smile lines?
Amount of Bellafill Needed
Jul 12, 2018
This is a very good and frequently asked question. Without an in office consultation it would not be possible to give a precise answer. One syringe of Bellafill may give you mild improvement however since it lasts, you can always add more Bellafill later on to add to the improvement. Many times the proper way to treat the frown lines is to lift the cheeks with Bellafill instead of injecting directly into the line.
When will adverse side effects of Dysport subside. Can this be muscle or nerve damage?
Dysport side effects
Jun 21, 2018
The effects of Dysport last 3 months on average. It does not sound like your issues are related to the Dysport injections but you should follow up with your injector. Good luck.
Bellafil instead of surgery for asymmetrical chin?
Bellafill for chin
Jun 13, 2018
Yes, Bellafill can be very helpful to produce symmetry in the chin. The advantage of this filler versus others is that it lasts for years instead of months. Good luck.
Do Bellafill fillers cause granulomas/lumps and migrate over time?
Bellafill granuloma
Jun 7, 2018
Hi. Thanks for your question. Bellafill is a wonderful long lasting filler which gives very nice, natural looking results. While rare, it is possible to develop a granuloma/lump from the injection. Should this develop, there are ways to treat it. If not treated, it does not move anywhere. Good luck.
Botox alternatives?
Botox alternative
Jun 6, 2018
Thanks for the question. In addition to Botox, Dysport and Xeomin can be used as well. If you still have movement after Botox injections, you should discuss this with your injector as it may be that the injection sites or the amount used may need to be adjusted. Alternatively, your injector may want to use one of the other products. Good luck.

Laser for facial lesions

Best form of laser resurfacing for Melasma.
Laser Treatment for Melasma
May 25, 2018
The treatment of melasma is challenging. Typically prior to laser treatment the condition should be treated with a bleaching agent along with a retinoid and sunscreen, preferably with iron oxide as a component. If there is no significant improvement, IPL or Q-Switched laser may provide some benefit.
