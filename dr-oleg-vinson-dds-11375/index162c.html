Is it ok to have a cryofacial a day after Botox?
Facial treatments after Botox
Jun 6, 2017
Thank you for your question.
Congratulations on getting Botox.
It takes up to two weeks for Botox to take full effect.
It is not advisable to do any facials, Hydrafacial, cryofacial, chemical peels, microdermabrasion, laser and etc. within first two weeks.
This is the advice I give to all my patients.
