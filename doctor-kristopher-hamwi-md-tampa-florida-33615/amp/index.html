Oct 27, 2020

How long are drains usually left in after mommy makeovers? (Tummy tuck)
Tummy tucks and drains
Oct 25, 2020
The general teaching in plastic surgery training is that most drains remain in place until the output is consistently less than 30ml. The reason you don’t want to remove a drain too early is that the body has trouble absorbing large volumes of fluid and you don’t wanting the fluid accumulating under the skin and forming a seroma. There are various ways to reduce your chances of developing a seroma, one of which is when a surgeon uses progressive tension sutures.
