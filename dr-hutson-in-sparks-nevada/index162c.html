Help! Has the stitch split from my labiaplasty?
Day 6 labia looks swollen.
May 28, 2018
From your day #6 photos, I think the labia minors just look swollen which is to be expected 6 days after surgery. Use ice and get off your feet to reduce swelling and enhance healing. Stop looking at it every day so it doesn’t stress you out. I think you will be really happy with the final results. Be patient with the healing process.
Is this prolapse?
Maybe.
May 27, 2018
It is hard to tell for sure looking at the photos, but it looks suspicious for a rectocele. A rectocele is a hernia of the rectum into the vagina. This is more likely to happen after a vaginal delivery.
I recommend seeing your OB/GYN for an evaluation and exam. This condition is not dangerous but may cause bothersome symptoms. Often, the symptoms can be overcome with good bowel control and regularity. Surgical correction restores normal anatomy and helps relieve symptoms.
Can laser correct some loose labial tissue? There’s some loose skin in the labial area, not a lot
Laser or radiofrequency energy may help.
May 27, 2018
It depends on the amount and location of the loose skin. Radio frequency energy may be better at shrinkiing and lifting the skin if it is healthy. If it is saggy secondary to atrophy with menopause then laser may work better to make the skin plumper and healthier, then RF to further optimize results if needed.
