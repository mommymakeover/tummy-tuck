Is a vampire facial relatively botch-proof?
Safety of Vampire Face Lift
Jan 22, 2019
A Vampire Face Lift is quite safe.  Since it is your own blood products, there is no chance of reaction to the product.  Placing the product does not require great precision like a dermal filler would.  Assuming that the area is cleaned properly and the blood is drawn and prepared properly, you shouldn’t have a problem.  All that being said, make sure that your injector has sufficient training and experience doing the procedure.

Hair removal for dark skin: Do laser treatments really work?
Laser Hair Removal and PCOS
Dec 23, 2017
Thank you for your question.  Laser Hair Removal is a very good option for patients with PCOS regardless of the skin color.  It will take multiple treatments.  Please make sure you have your treatments done by a physician with a lot of experience working with darker skin.  Also, make sure that the laser they are using is really for hair removal.  Not all lasers are equal!  Some lasers are better for hair removal than others.  I hope this helps.  Good luck!  Dr. DiPasquale

Swelling and Lip Augmentation
