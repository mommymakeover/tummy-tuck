Statement: Dr. David Gerth is a board-eligible plastic surgeon in Miami Beach, Florida. From a young age he was drawn to a science-forward career, which ultimately led him to the field of aesthetic medicine. He specializes in plastic surgery of the face, breast, and body. He finds it deeply rewarding to help his patients improve the quality of their life.

After obtaining his undergraduate degree in genetics at the University of Georgia, Dr. Gerth pursued his medical degree at the University of Tennessee Health Science Center in Memphis, Tennessee. Following medical school, he completed a residency in ear, nose, and throat (ENT), also at the University of Tennessee Health Science Center, and subsequently completed a fellowship in facial plastic surgery with the Glasgold Group in Highland Park, New Jersey.

Dr. Gerth’s medical training makes him uniquely equipped to perform facial plastic and reconstructive procedures. The face is arguably the most challenging part of the body because of the fragile bone and tissue structure. Dr. Gerth applies the same deftly handed skill set when performing plastic surgery procedures on the breast and body.

He became a plastic surgeon because he wanted to fuse his love of science with his creativity and imagination. The art of reshaping a face or body requires both facets, to be successful. Additionally, he has a passion for meeting and interacting with people from all walks of life. This has helped him connect with his patients, who ultimately put their trust in him.

Dr. Gerth has hospital privileges at Mount Sinai Medical Center in New York City and Mercy Hospital, Kendall Regional Medical Center, and The Memorial Health System in Miami, among others.
