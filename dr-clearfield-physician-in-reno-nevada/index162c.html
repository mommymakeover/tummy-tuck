Dr. William Clearfield, a graduate of LaSalle College, the College of Osteopathic Medicine and Surgery, Des Moines, IA., completed a rotating internship and served as an OB/GYN resident Metropolitan Hospital, Philadelphia, PA. Certified by the American Board of Family Medicine after completing a Family Practice resident in 1982 at United Health and Hospital Services in Kingston, PA, Dr. Clearfield, since 1982, is a leading figure in integrative and aesthetic medicine. With graduate expertise in areas ranging from Cardiac Rehabilitation (the University of Wisconsin/Lacrosse) to Medical Acupuncture (UCLA, 1991), to Age Management and Non-Surgical Aesthetic Medicine, (Fellowship trained and Diplomat status from the American Academy of Anti-Aging Medicine,) to non-surgical facial aesthetics (AAFE trained), Dr. C is the leading authority on cutting-edge medicine in Northern Nevada. In the field of aesthetics, Dr. Clearfield was an early (2004) proponent of laser treatments for hair removal, skin tightening, and facial resurfacing. He is trained in mesotherapy, advanced botulinum toxin, dermal filler, PDO thread, PRP, and PRF interventions.

He is a big believer in radiofrequency for vaginal and skin rejuvenation and recently acquired EVOKE,a bipolar radiofrequency device for a no-needle, no anesthetic, facial and chin remodeling and fat dissolution.

But wait, there’s more! Dr. Clearfield is considered THE expert in bioidentical hormone replacement therapy in Northern Nevada. His lectures on topics such as the Endocrinology of Traumatic Brain Injury, the Endocrinology of Libido, and the Endocrinology of Autism, make him a highly sought after speaker at OMED, the American Osteopathic Association’s Scientific Convention, NOMA, the Nevada Osteopathic Association, the American Osteopathic Society of Rheumatic Diseases, the International Congress of Alternative and Complementary Medicine, the Age Management Medical Group, and the American Academy of Anti-Aging Medicine. Dr. Clearfield is also one of three Nevada delegates to the American Osteopathic Association’s House of Delegates and the Executive Director of the American Osteopathic Society of Rheumatic Diseases.

He recently welcomed his first class of students to the Clearfield Training Program in aesthetic medicine and hormone replacement therapy.
