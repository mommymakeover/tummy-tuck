Doctors

Şule Güngör, MD

Name: Şule Güngör, MD
Statement: Professor Şule Güngör, MD is an experienced dermatologist on hair transplants, skin diseases and cosmetic dermatology.
After she obtained her medical degree at 2001, she had specialty in medicine at Dermatology at 2006 followed by Associate Professor of Dermatology at 2016, Professor of Dermatology at October 2021.

She has 20 years of professional expertise in Dermatology and Hair loss treatments.
Based in Istanbul, Turkey at Prof Dr Şule Güngör Clinic, she provides and administer the most thoughtful and effective treatments for the patients looking for hair transplants & skin treatments.

As a dermatology professor and one of the most competent names in her field, Professor Dr Şule examines and diagnoses the causes of hair loss in patients.
Although hair transplantation is a permanent treatment for hair loss, it is not suitable for all hair loss types. So, the first step required for a successful hair transplant is the correct diagnosis made by a specialist doctor. After the diagnosis, donor hair capacity, degree of baldness and meeting the patient’s expectation from hair transplantation are the most important factors in evaluating suitability for hair transplantation. After examining the patient’s anamnesis and hair condition, Prof Dr. Şule Güngör decides on the patient’s suitability for hair transplantation.
When it comes to the transplantation stage, she carries out the operations with her team that has full experience and competence in hair transplantation methods; FUE Hair Transplant, Sapphire FUE Hair transplant, DHI Hair transplant.

While having a treatment with Prof Şule patients will notice that safety and health is the first priority. Safety comes with doctors and her team’s expertise, the quality of medical devices, the safety of methods used and sterile environment. Hair transplant operations are carried out at the fully equipped, spacious medical center prepared for hair transplantation operations and licensed by the Ministry of Health.

The post-transplantation stage is critical, the reaction of each skin is unique to the person. Providing healing treatments suitable for the patient’s skin after transplantation, and 1 year follow up are other privileged services provided by Professor Şule.

If you are looking for a solution for your hair loss, first of all, you need to get the right information. To begin your journey, schedule a free consultation with Prof. Şule.
