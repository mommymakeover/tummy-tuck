What is my best option for mole removal? I want both moles on my cheek and nose removed.
Always a chance for scarring
Mar 19, 2015
It is in your best interest to have any "mole" that is removed sent for pathology. There are Amelanotic (no pigment) melanomas that can sometimes fool you. As far as not wanting a scar, there is ALWAYS a chance of permanent scarring with any surgical procedure no matter who performs it. You could be trading one mark for another.
