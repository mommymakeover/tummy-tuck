Stick and Poke tattoos are easy to remove.

Stick and poke tattoos are usually very easy to remove with few treatments. Make sure you find a professional that has a picolaser as this will enable you to remove the tattoo even faster. Best of luck.
Tattoo removal gone wrong! Raised scarring. Any treatments to flatten or tighten?
The answer can be complex
Aug 9, 2018
Thanks for posting the photos. I see this all the time in my market and yes, the scarring is the result of a poorly trained technician using a Q-switch laser. The burns and scarring are permanent. What is needed in this situation is to get rid of the rest of the ink as best as possible. In addition, a good picosecond laser can be used to soften the scar tissue up. The Enlighten3 laser from Cutera is very well suited for this type of treatment although there are others on the market now....
