Cost of Botox
Dec 21, 2017
Botox is generally sold by the unit and can vary in price from $10 to $13 per unit in our area. The number of units needed per treatment can range from as few as 10 units to upwards of 60 units depending on the number of areas treated. Allergan offers a program (Brilliant Distinctions) which tracks any Botox, Filler, or CoolSculpting purchase to create discount coupons for future use which helps to keep the cost down.
