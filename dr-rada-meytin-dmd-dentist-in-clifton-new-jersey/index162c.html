Can you perform an Oxygen Jet Peel Facial after dermal fillers? Will it harm the fillers?
Facial Treatments After Fillers
Jun 12, 2018
Thank you for bringing your question to RealSelf! You should wait at least two weeks after having dermal fillers injected before having any facial treatments done. Most fillers are composed of a hyaluronic acid gel, that can be malleable for several days after placement. You can also experience swelling and/or bruising for up to two weeks after filler placement, and treatments like facials, peels, or exfoliation can exacerbate this. Good luck from NJ!
