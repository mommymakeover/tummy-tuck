Can a patient have a fat transfer to the breast and Sculptra?
Sculptra
Jun 19, 2018
Thank you for your question. Yes, is possible to have a fat transfer to the breast along with Sculptra. Depending on your cosmetic goal and desired results, Sculptra can be used to add volume in the breast. Unlike other dermal fillers, Sculptra helps your body produce collagen over time and add volume to desired areas. Please make sure you get a formal evaluation from a specialist who has experience in this type of procedure and the appropriate board certifications.
