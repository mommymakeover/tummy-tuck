Dr. Hensel is a published author in plastic surgery journals and textbooks, and has given numerous lectures on the various aspects of plastic surgery. Dr. Hensel was appointed as a clinical instructor in surgery at the Northeastern Ohio Universities College of Medicine in 1999.
You can be confident in choosing Dr. Hensel as your surgeon knowing that he is board certified in plastic surgery by the American Board of Plastic Surgery. He was also named as The Best and most trusted Plastic Surgeon in Charleston for 2008, 2009, 2010, 2011, 2012 and 2013. He was also featured on national television performing a breast augmentation and facial fillers. This show, MTV True Lives aired in April of 2010!

There are many factors to consider when returning to work after a Tummy Tuck. It really have a lot to do with what you do for a living.
