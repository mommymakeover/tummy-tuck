Can Juvederm Add Volume to Sunken Cheeks?
Juvederm Voluma is a great product for adding volume back into the midface.
Mar 22, 2016
Juvederm Voluma is a great product for adding volume back into the midface. It is thicker than regular Juvederm, and made specifically for volumization. It lasts for up to two years, and patients have had a very high satisfaction rate with it. I suggest visiting a board-certified dermatologist for a consultation to see if you are a good candidate for this product.
I had my lips injected today with Juvederm and am wondering if appearance is due to swelling?
Juvederm can be dissolved if needed.
Mar 22, 2016
8 hours after Juvederm injections to the lips, you are very likely swollen. Swelling from lip injections can last for up to two weeks, so just be patient and evaluate your final results after two weeks have passed. If your lips are still uneven after that time, visit your injector again to see what can be done. Juvederm can be dissolved if needed.
Is There Any Way to Make Botox Wear off Faster?
Unfortunately, there is no way to make Botox wear off faster.
Mar 22, 2016
Unfortunately, there is no way to make Botox wear off faster. Generally, results will last from 3-4 months. I don’t know exactly what you don’t like about your results, but you may consider visiting your injector again to see if there’s any way to correct what you don’t like.
Does Botox Work on Horizontal Forehead Creases?
Yes, Botox is often used to treat horizontal creases on the forehead.
Mar 22, 2016
Yes, Botox is often used to treat horizontal creases on the forehead. This is one of the main 3 areas that Botox is considered best for, the other two being frown lines and crow’s feet. Make sure you see an experienced, board-certified dermatologist for your Botox treatment in order to get the best, most natural looking results.
Do I Really Need Double Dysport to Get Same Effect of Botox?
Yes, more units of Dysport are needed to get the same effect as Botox.
Mar 22, 2016
Yes, more units of Dysport are needed to get the same effect as Botox. In fact the ratio is slightly higher than double, at 2.5 or 3:1. Usually the prices will end up being comparable, since Dysport is cheaper per unit than Botox but you need more units of it. 
How Long Does Dysport Last for Wrinkles on the Forehead?
Yes, it is possible to have multiple areas of concern treated at the same time with Dysport.
Mar 22, 2016
Dysport lasts approximately 3-4 months in most patients. Yes, it is possible to have multiple areas of concern treated at the same time with Dysport; in fact, it is a very common practice to treat the forehead, glabella (frown lines between eyebrows), and crow’s feet at the corner of the eyes at the same time. I suggest visiting a board-certified dermatologist for a consultation and custom Dysport treatment.
Dysport in the Same Quantity As Botox for the Same Result?
No, the number of units required are different between Botox and Dysport.
Mar 22, 2016
No, the number of units required are different between Botox and Dysport. Generally, the ratio of Dysport to Botox for the same effect is 2.5 or 3:1. However, practices take this into account when pricing, so the cost for Dysport or Botox should be approximately the same. Always make sure to have your injections done by a board-certified dermatologist with experience using these treatments.
