I want to remove this tattoo completely. How should I do it?
Pocosecond tattoo removal
Sep 14, 2016
Picosecond tattoo removal can be more effective than older nanosecond lasers. There are three brands of picosure lasers although there is no comparison of the three lasers. The best wavelength for black ink is the 1064 which is now available on all three systems. Expect a minimum of four sessions eight weeks apart .
When to stop shaving and exfoliating before laser hair removal session?
Shaving prior to laser hair removal
Sep 14, 2016
Do not shave for two days prior to laser hair removal. The hair needs to be clipped prior to the laser treatment.

Can I get laser hair removal on face done after/during laser resurfacing?
Hair removal after resurfacing.
Sep 14, 2016
Laser hair removal requires 4-8 see ions at monthly intervals. You can perform laser hair removal safely after 8 weeks .If hair is the more distressing issue you could finish series of hair removal first. 
Allergic reaction to laser tattoo removal?
Reaction to aggressive laser treatment
Sep 14, 2016
It is best to be careful on first laser treatment especially on a 8 week old tattoo. Treatment with a topical or if necessary oral steroid will calm down reaction. Wait at least 8 weeks before other session and if necessary do half of tattoo and do other half a few weeks later to Decrease reaction.
