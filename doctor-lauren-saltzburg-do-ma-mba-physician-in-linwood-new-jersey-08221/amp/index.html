Statement: Dr. Lauren Saltzburg, DO, MA, MBA is the founder and chief surgeon of Luminary Cosmetic Medicine & Hair Restoration in Linwood, New Jersey. In addition to offering a wide range of hair restoration treatments, including FUE hair transplants, she and the staff at her Jersey Shore-area practice perform a variety of cosmetic procedures, such as injectables and dermal fillers, laser skin resurfacing, and laser vaginal tightening. Dr. Saltzburg is recognized for her warm and caring approach to treatment.

Dr. Saltzburg received her medical degree from the Philadelphia College of Osteopathic Medicine while simultaneously pursuing her MBA in Healthcare Administration and master’s degree in Biomedical Ethics from St. Joseph’s University, the only PCOM graduate ever to do so.

Dr. Saltzburg is an active member of multiple professional organizations, including the International Society of Hair Restoration Surgery. Passionate about advancing the field of hair restoration, Dr. Saltzburg spends much of her free time attending conferences around the country to further her knowledge, as well as training aspiring hair restoration physicians in cutting-edge techniques.
