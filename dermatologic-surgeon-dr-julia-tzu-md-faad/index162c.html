Is there a treatment that can help with my acne and acne scars?
Puled dye laser
Sep 29, 2020
Early stage reddish acne scarring, also known as acne erythema, can be improved with pulsed dye laser treatments. For later stage acne scarring that shows up as skin colored skin depressions, acne scar subcision rotated with microneedling can help. Multiple treatments are needed for best results. Make sure you seek a qualified board certified dermatologist with experience in acne scarring to treat your condition.
What kind of treatments would work for my pitted acne scars?
Subcision with filler plus microneedling
Sep 29, 2020
It appears you have a combination of rolling, boxcar, and ice pick scars. In my experience, rotating treatments of subcision (with fillers) and microneedling can result in significant improvements in most people with similar acne scarring. Several treatments of both are needed for optimum outcomes. Make sure you find a qualified board certified dermatologist who has experience with acne scar treatments.
