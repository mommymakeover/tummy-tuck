How to approach doctor about placing nipples too high?
nipple repositioning
Sep 19, 2023
A physical exam is needed to give you advice. Generally the position of the nipple is marked in the Pre-op area with the patient standing. Multiple physical findings influence the nipple position. These include how much laxity in the breast, the distance of the nipple from the sternal notch, the natural volume of the patient's breast."Stargazing" issue is typically due to the loss of elasticity of the breast skin and also influenced by the weight of the breast with or without implants...
Options for neck lift at age 33? Sagging in lower face & neck after Ultherapy
sagging neck skin
Sep 11, 2023
A thorough exam is needed to give you the best advice for your concerns. However, you may have improvement with the S-lift, it is a limited neck lift.The neck skin can still wrinkle when you tilt you chin downward.
Age 32, what procedure would correct the puffiness and shadows under my eyes?
eyelid puffiness
Sep 11, 2023
Generally, puffiness under the lower eyelaches is associated with fat in that area that is bulging. A physical exam can clarify that. If that is puffiness from fat then a blepharoplasty can correct this issue.
Which procedure would be best for me to take to improve my recessed chin and lack of a jawline? (Ph
genioplasty
Aug 21, 2023
Complete exam of your dental "bite" is necessary. A possible option is a Chin implant. The implant will give you jaw line more projection and therefore improve your profile. During your exam you may discover even more options.
Is the issue with my face from volume loss or sagging tissue?
cheek lift best option
Aug 21, 2023
Full exam is necessary. An option is a sub periosteal mid-face (cheek lift). This involves surgically lifting your entire cheek, vertically. This method reversers the vertical drop of your cheek soft tissues that causes the Nasolabial fold to get fuller and the cheek bone to look flatter due to the aging process. With that surgery fat can be used to further improve your cheeks.

Neck (chin) liposuction

Do I have gynecomastia or do I just need to lose some weight?
gynecomastia
Aug 13, 2023
After a complete exam for gynecomastia I typically recommend a combination of excision of the breast tissue and liposuction of the chest. This approach will remove the breast tissue that is under the areola and the liposuction will smooth out the chest contour after the breast tissue is removed. On very thin males, maybe just the breast tissue excision is adequate.
Fat grafting or implant to augment lips?
lip augmentation
Aug 13, 2023
Fat vs lip implant. The more reliable procedure I have experienced is using an Alloderm tissue implant for the lip enhancement.
Blepharoplasty or brow lift? Can I do well with just eyelid surgery?
brow lift and blepharoplasty complement each
Aug 13, 2023
The brow lift improvement can be simulated by looking into a mirror and lift your brow upward. You will probably see improvement and may still benefit from the upper blepharoplasty. However, depending on your physical exam, the upper blepharoplasty may be ok. With either operation I typically recommend removing the frown muscles.
Very round face, unsure of what procedure is best for my jawline?
chin liposuction
Aug 13, 2023
The term "chin liposuction" typically refers to liposuction of the neck just under the chin. The photo indicates that procedure will be very beneficial for your neck contour. A physical exam needs to be done to determine what is necessary for each patient.
In my 20s, what are my options for improving the appearance of my lower face?
chin augmentation
Aug 13, 2023
I recommend a chin implant for patients with a small chin that is apparent when you get a side face profile photo.
An exam can determine if that procedure is good for you.
Am I a good candidate for a mid facelift?
Sub periosteal midface lift and lower blepharoplasty
Aug 9, 2023
I have found the sub periosteal mid-facelift and lower blepharoplasty allows for the cheek to be lifted up correcting the lower eyelid flatness. A full examination is needed to give you a more comprehensive recommendation for your concerns.
Would I benefit from facelift/ neck and eyelid surgery?
Midface lift and eyelid surgery
Aug 9, 2023
The central face aging gives patients a tired look. By doing a lower eyelid with a sub periosteal mid face lift will correct the flatness under your eyes and reposition the lower cheek fat over your cheeks giving you a very fresh, natural youthful look.The SMAS neck lift combined the with midface further enhance the lower face and neck.
