Lipo or CoolSculpting waiting time after a mommy makeover, how long should I wait?
Liposuction after mommy makeover

When the swelling has gone down enough, your wounds have healed well, and your energy level is back to normal, then you are ready for next treatment. More than likely you can get CoolSculpting done a bit sooner than liposuction.
