What treatment would you recommend for these rolling acne scars?
Bellafill use for acne scars
Feb 3, 2017
Bellafill works well for these types of scars known as rolling scars. These scars are usually shallow and disappear when you stretch the skin around them just as you demonstrate in your photos.. Bellafill is FDA approved for this purpose and may be an excellent option for you. I suggest you consult a dermatologist who has experience treating acne scars and the use of fillers such as Bellafill.
50 year old female. I have rolling scars. I want to improve the appearance.
Rolling Scar treatment options
Jan 13, 2017
I would suggest you consider combination treatment including IPL to treat the underlying redness. Along with this you may benefit from subsicion of the scars followed by injection of Bellafill . You may also get improvement by adding microneedling or radiofrequency to stimulate collagen production which may give you a smoother and more even complexion.
