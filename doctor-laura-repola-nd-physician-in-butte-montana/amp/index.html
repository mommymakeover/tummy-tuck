Can I have Botox & lip fillers 24 hours before being sedated for a tummy procedure?
Sure but why not wait?
Mar 5, 2021
It is not contraindicated to receive those treatments within 24 hrs of a tummy tuck so the bottom line is yes. But I would recommend getting aesthetics treatments at least 2 weeks before or 2 weeks after any major surgery. It just gives us a little room to get the treatment right before adding more stress to your body. I can’t think of any thing that could go wrong, but I also can’t come up with a good reason not to allow for time for these treatments to be done when you are feeling…
I just had kybella and want to schedule treatment for Botox and have threading. How long should I wait
Let Kybella do its thing!
Mar 5, 2021
You can have Botox at the same time or anytime after Kybella, without a waiting period. Go for it. But if the threads you want are in the area of the Kybella, wait for it to do its thing. You may see some changes in the area of the Kybella treatment to the chin that affect your needs for threading to that area.
Is CoolSculpting an effective treatment to reduce armpit fat?
Bye bye bra fat
Feb 16, 2021
Yes CoolSculpting is effective in the area of unwanted and stubborn bra fat. It usually takes two treatments 6 weeks apart for the best outcomes.
