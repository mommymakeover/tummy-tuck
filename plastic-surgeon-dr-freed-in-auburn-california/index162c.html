Why do some people's neck appear wider after a facelift?
Facelift recovery
Jun 30, 2021
It is common after a facelift to have some swelling in the neck which gives it a wide appearance. This can also happen in the temporal areas after a mini facelift. These resolve without any intervention, although there are a few tricks that can help them resolve faster.

Facelift longevity
Jun 29, 2021
Facelift usually last around 10-15 years. The best ways to improve the longevity of a facelift is a medical grade skin care routine. Regularly seeing an esthetician can also increase the longevity of your facelift. Sunscreen and sun protection will be the most important key to this.

Unless it can still be palpated, I would not recommend having it dissolved because it is likely already absorbed.

Is it abnormal that I’m still feeling tight in my neck and ears?
Recovery after facelift
Jun 25, 2021
It can be common to have a tight feeling after lower facelift. Sometimes a small amount of scar will form causing this feeling. The most common place for this is under your chin/neck area. If this happens, we can inject a small amount of steroids to loosen the scar without loosening the skin and relieve that feeling. We can also teach you how to massage to break up scar tissue formation. Even if you did nothing, this feeling usually resolves without any intervention with time.
How do people with dry eyes have a facelift?
Facelift and dry eyes
Jun 25, 2021
Having a facelift should not effect your dry eyes. You will likely need to continue using your drops. If you decided to have eyelid surgery on top of a facelift, this could cause come increased irritation of your eyes initially. We would provide you with ointment and drops to help with this. Facelift alone usually does not cause changes in dry eyes.
