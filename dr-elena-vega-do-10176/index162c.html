Am I a candidate for liposuction?
Aug 16, 2018
You can definitely have laser liposuction done, but it will do nothing for the loose skin you have at the bottom of your abdomen.  You would have the best result from an abdominoplasty and liposuction to the waist and back.  Be sure you discuss your expectations with your surgeon prior to surgery so you are not disappointed a few months later if things don’t turn out the way you had envisioned.  
Cost, recovery/down time, scarring?
Perfect for liposuction!
Aug 14, 2018
You are a great candidate for liposuction.  Yes, the incisions can be placed inside the umbilicus and will not be visualized.  Downtime is typically a day or two depending on your threshold for pain.  The cost of the procedure is dependent on the office you go to.  I typically charge 2000 dollars an area plus an operating room fee.  It is best to have a few consultations with multiple surgeons in order to see which one is the right fit for you!
