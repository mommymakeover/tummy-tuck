Is Botox a good treatment for droopy upper eyelids?
Botox for Droopy Eyelids
Jun 20, 2018
Great question! Botox is definitely used to help lift the brows and the eyelids. One has to treat the glabella, procerus and the lateral canthus (crows feet). If you do suffer from droopy eyelids, the injector has to be very conservative when treating the forehead lines because Botox in the forehead lines can cause the brows and lids to become heavy. So, my recommendation is to start conservative and go with an experienced injector.
Do I get the same dose on my third treatment of jaw Botox?
Dosing for Masseter Muscle
Jun 20, 2018
Your dose for the masseter muscle which helps slim the jawline depends on the strength of your muscle during contraction and the injector. A full dose for treating the masseter muscle in our office will vary from 30u to 50u of Botox depending on the muscle size and strength during contraction.
