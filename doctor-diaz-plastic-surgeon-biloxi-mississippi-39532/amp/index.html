Rhinoplasty
Oct 27, 2020
I would suggest that you do research to find a board certified plastic surgeon in your area and set-up a consultation with them to get their opinion. Each surgeon will have a different idea of what they think will look best, so doing your research is important.
High BP week of surgery. Is this something I need to worry about?
High Blood Pressure Before Surgery
Oct 27, 2020
Controlled blood pressure is essential prior to performing cosmetic surgery. It may be a good idea to consult with your general practitioner to identify any underlying causes.
Time between procedures?
Wait Time Between Surgeries
Oct 27, 2020
I generally like to wait 1-2 weeks between cosmetic surgeries requiring general anesthesia. Each board certified plastic surgeon has their own requirements/preferences.
Advice for Exercise After Breast Augmentation?
Exercise after a breast augmentation
Mar 29, 2018
We require our patients not to participate in any form of exercise for 4 weeks after the breast augmentation surgery. This includes any type of exercise, including those that only work the legs. The repetitious movement alone on a elliptical machine can be enough to cause trauma to the tissue surrounding the implant. We prefer that our patients wait. Hope this helps.
How Do You Massage Breasts After Breast Augmentation?
Massaging breast is a thing of the past
Mar 29, 2018
The proper technique and approach used today for a breast augmentation by a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon does not require any breast massage. Just the movement of your arm during your daily activities is all you need.

Breast Augmentation and weight loss after pregnancy
Mar 21, 2017
Yes- you should plan to loose the baby weight prior to breast augmentation surgery. Your breast can change from pregnancy, breast feeding, weight gain or weight loss. In order to achieve the results you have in mind; a stable breast base size helps us determine what implant size would be best suited to satisfy the desired outcome. Once you have reached a stable weight you are happy with- plan a few cosmetic consultations with qualified plastic surgeons.
