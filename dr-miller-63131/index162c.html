Can I use Benzaclin on my face and get laser hair removal in my armpits?
BPO and Laser Hair Reduction
Jul 18, 2019
You are absolutely fine do have LHR in the underarms while using Benzaclin on your face. The benzyl peroxide in benzaclin can cause photosensitivity but usually the photosensitivity occurs in the areas where the medication is placed (or close by). The underarms will not become photosensitized by BPO on the face. Go for it–you will love not having to deal with armpit hair!
Best regards,
Dr. Amy
