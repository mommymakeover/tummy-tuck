Can I get PRP for hair loss with Factor V Leiden?
It is safe to perform PRP for Hair loss in your case
Apr 13, 2022
Hello there! There are no formal contraindications for PRP hair in Factor V Leiden carriers. The main contraindications would be localized infection/ inflammation or current infectious or inflammatory systemic disease. For best results, it is also important to rule out secondary hair loss causes such as vitamin and nutritional deficiencies, anemia, hypothyroid and other chronic diseases. At times a scalp biopsy is needed to confirm hair loss cause and determine if PRP is the right treatment…

Number of session depends on each patient individually

Hello there! At our practice, we perform 1 session every four weeks. Total number of sessions varies for each patient, but usually ranges from 4-8 sessions. Some patients experience improvement even after one session, but for long lasting results, we recommend a full course of treatments as above and then one maintenance session every 6-12 months.

Can I get PRP injection or microneedling post Covid 19?
Safe timelines for PRP post-COVID
Mar 29, 2022
Hello there! Since PRP is obtained from your own blood, it should be safe for administration following infections or vaccinations.
