When to expect clear vision after PRK?
Dry Eyes
Aug 11, 2018
If you have dry eyes, there are other options to try. Punctal plugs can be inserted right in the doctor's office. There are also prescription eye drops for dry eyes. Good luck
I am looking into getting refractive surgery for my high prescription. Am I a candidate?
LASIK
Jul 27, 2018
All else being normal, you would have enough residual corneal tissue in the bed for femtosecond custom LASIK. PRK is also an option, but there is a slight risk of corneal haze post-op with such a high correction. Visian lens implants are also an option for you, especially if you are concerned with corneal surgery. Good luck
My cornea thickness is 470 micrones and have myopia of -6 in left and -5 in right eye, am I a ca
PRK
Jul 1, 2018
Current standard are to leave at least 300 microns of corneal tissue behind to maintain proper corneal strength and integrity. If you underwent PRK for your current correction, you would have more 300 microns left. All else being normal, it appears you would be a candidate. Good luck.
Recently I had IPL and it was so painful I couldn’t complete the session. Is this typical?
Pain with IPL
Jun 24, 2018
Typically, the energy of the IPL can be adjusted lower until the patient is comfortable. Many IPL hand pieces also cool the surface of the skin during treatment. The skin can be numbed with topical medication ahead of time. For IPL hair removal, it's important to minimize the hair on the surface of the skin. Good luck.
My child hit me in my open eye. I had PRK 20 days ago and my vision is still blurry quite a bit.
Pain after eye trauma
Jun 19, 2018
You may have developed a corneal surface epithelial defect from getting hit in the eye. This could have happen from the trauma alone, even if you didn't have PRK. You should be examined by you eye doctor to determine whether the eye was injured.
I have -1.25 eyesight. Can I suitable for laser surgery?
Laser vision correction
Jun 10, 2018
Your prescription alone is not the only factor that will determine if you are a candidate for laser vision correction. Extensive testing, including corneal mapping and examination by your doctor will be necessary. Also, if you are over 40 and take your glasses off the read, you may require reading glasses after laser vision correction. Good luck.
I had PRK surgery 6 weeks ago and for the last 3 days my left eye has been aching and is red. Is
PRK
Jun 9, 2018
After 6 weeks the surface of the cornea should be completely healed. You may have another condition unrelated to your recent surgery. You should return to your eye doctor to have it checked. Good luck.
