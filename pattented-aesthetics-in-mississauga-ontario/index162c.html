RF microneedling after Laser sclerotherapy?
RF microneedling after laser Sclerotherapy
May 9, 2023
Thanks for your question. I would recommend treating the vascular lesions (spider veins) first and then wait a month to 6 weeks before having RF microneedling. This will give your body enough time to heal and clear the appearance of the veins- and your skin will be much more even overall after the RF microneedling series (usually 3-4 treatments spaced 1 month apart). Hope this helps!
Can I have Microbotox before CO2 Laser Fractional?
Micro Botox before fractional laser treatments
May 9, 2023
Thanks for your question! I recommend waiting two weeks after the microbotox to have the laser treatment so you can see some results and hopefully clear up some active lesions. In my experience the fractional laser will help with pore size and oil control as well. Hope this helps!
