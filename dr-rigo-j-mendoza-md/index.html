How is the clitoral hood reduced?

Liposuction botched my vagina. Can I fix this?
Liposuction botched my vagina. Can I fix this?
Oct 20, 2022
First off, I tell all my patients that liposuction removes fat - it DOES NOT tighten skin. One of the most common risks of liposuction is loose skin. I use Renuvion (J-Plasma), on most of my lipos to help with skin tightening. Once you have loose skin like in your pictures, the only treatment is a mini TT / pubic lift.
Pubic lipo or lift for flat genital area?
Pubic lipo or lift for flat genital area?
Oct 20, 2022
It's hard to tell from the picture which option would be better for you. We typically will do an aggressive lipo followed by Renuvion (J-Plasma) of the areas to aid in skin tightening. A lot of times, in addition to the pubic lift that you had done, you have to make incisions along the inner thigh crease to get rid of excess skin in a horizontal direction as well.
Can you have monsplasty surgery with fat transfer to the hips?
Can you have monsplasty surgery with fat transfer to the hips?
Oct 20, 2022
Yes, you can certainly have fat transfer to the hips and a monsplasty done at the same time. We frequently will perform liposuction, monsplasty or mini TT and fat transfer at the same time. It can also be done with local /tumescent anesthesia only.
Monsplasty - Should you always have drains with this surgery?
Monsplasty - Should you always have drains with this surgery?
Oct 20, 2022
Unlike a full tummy tuck where you may need to do extensive undermining of the skin, it is not required for a Monsplasty or mini TT. Therefore, rarely if ever, will you need to put drains in a Monsplasty or mini TT.
Type of results to expect from Ultherapy if I have Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome and hypermobility joint s
Type of results to expect from Ultherapy if I have Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome
Oct 20, 2022
I wouldn't recommend Ultherapy with Ehlers-Danlos Syndrome. Since EDS causes the skin to be stretchy and fragile, you probably would not see much result and it certainly would last too long.
Can Ultherapy glow treatments around the mouth cause fat loss?
Can Ultherapy glow treatments around the mouth cause fat loss?
Oct 20, 2022
Glow treatment utilizes the most superficial treatment head. Therefore, it should not penetrate deep enough to effect the fat layer. If anything, you should do a full treatment to target collagen stimulation in the deeper collagen.

Our preferred treatment method for this is an Ultherapy treatment (not just glow), to stimulate the deep collagen and a Plasma Pen treatment to target the superficial fine lines.

Will HIFU/ultheraphy treatment affect lip filler?
Will HIFU/ultheraphy treatment affect lip filler?
Oct 20, 2022
Ultherapy can be performed on different areas of the face that contain fillers at any time with no issues. However, if filler are done in an area that is going to be treated with Ultherapy, we recommend waiting at least a month after the filler treatment. Lip filler should not be a problem since you don't treat right on the lips with Ultherapy.
Reluctant but hopeful - is it true results might take 12 months?
Is it true results might take 12 months?
Oct 20, 2022
Most people start seeing improvement in 3 months. Definitely by 6 months you should see some results. There are some people that continue to see some improvement for up to 9 - 12 months. You mentioned that they "did many passes". Usual treatment requires 2 passes for each head that is used. The key is reading the US image correctly, so that you use the head for the correct depth of your collagen.
