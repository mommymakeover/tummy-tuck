I’m getting fillers in my face, will I be able to get a Venus freeze soon after?
Fillers and Venus Freeze
Jul 14, 2019
If you are scheduled to have the Venus Freeze then the fillers you should be ok. However, one thing to consider is the internal temperature of the skin when placing the fillers. We typically do not preform these 2 treatments on the same day. I would recommend you discuss your concerns with your injector prior to your appointment.
Regrowth within 4 to 5 days after laser treatment during my 4th, 5th and 6th sessions?
Laser hair removal regrowth
Jul 14, 2019
Typically laser hair removal on the body is preformed every 6-8 weeks to start and then decreased to 4-6 weeks as the hair becomes more sparing. Treatments are based on the hair growth cycle. The body has a longer cycle than the face. Maybe the treatments are too close? I would recommend talking with your provider about your concerns. Good luck
