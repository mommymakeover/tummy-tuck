Fresh look

Prp/prf is a great long term(2-5 years)solution in helping the body stimulate collagen , but it take a series of 3-6 treatments to visually see results. What has caused hard lumps on my lumps, was I botched?
Lip lumps
Apr 23, 2018
Lumps and bumps are quite common after lip filler injections, they usually resolve within a few weeks with mild massage. You can dissolve these lumps very quickly with hyaluronidase if a hyaluronic acid (restalyne/juvederm/revanesse) filler was used, but I would wait at least 2 weeks. All clinics or injectors using such product should have the reversing agent on standby at all times. Good luck from sin city .
How many treatments of PRP in the eye troughs are needed initially?
PRP under-eye
Apr 19, 2018
I think PRP is a great idea for your case to build collagen and help fill the tear trough, but will need at least 3 sessions about 4-6 weeks apart to start with, and probably 4-6 sessions overall. The results will be long term meaning a few years, and you will require ongoing maintenance sessions 1-2 times per year to keep desired results. I would also combine PRP with ultrasound and radiofrequency for best results. Good luck from Las Vegas .
