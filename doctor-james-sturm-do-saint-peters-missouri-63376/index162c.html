Solution for age spot?
Solution to age spots
Jan 18, 2018
Age spots ( commonly known as liver spots ) should be examined before embarking on a definitive treatment plan to make sure there is no evidence of skin cancer. Treatment options can include bleaching creams, chemical peels, dermabrasion and laser / intense pulsed light therapy. Use of a broad spectrum sun screen is advised to prevent worsening or reoccurrence of the age spots once treated. Best wishes.
