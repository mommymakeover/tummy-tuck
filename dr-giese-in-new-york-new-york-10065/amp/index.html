Ozempic vs. Wegovy vs. Mounjaro vs. Rybelsus comparison - What's best?
Differences in GLP-1 peptides
Nov 16, 2023
The active ingredient of Ozempic and Wegovy is semaglutide. Wegovy goes to higher doses and is FDA approved for weight loss. Rybelsus is the pill form and is generally thought not to give as strong appetite suppression. Mounjaro acts on 2 receptors and I think is best reserved for people with insulin resistance or diabetes type 2.
Will I gain weight back if I stop Ozempic?
Will I regain weight if stop Ozempic
Nov 16, 2023
I have many non-obese patients who have taken semaglutide to lose 10-20 pounds. Once they are stable at the target weight I wean them off. I have patients who are stable at that target weight for over a year now. It seems they have achieved a new set point. I think if a patient is starting at 50 pounds or more over their normal weight, I like to keep them on a maintenance dose longer because their chances of regaining the weight are higher.
Will Ozempic dissolve/metabolize the fat injections/dermal fillers in my face faster?
Weight loss with Ozempic and fat grafting to face
Nov 16, 2023
When someone loses weight, by any method, all fat cells in the body will shrink. If weight is gained they get larger. The same goes for fat grafting to the face. I would recommend doing it when you are close to your target weight.
For jawline & neck area is Smart, ultrasound or Laser lipo recommended or safe?
Ultrasound use for neck lipo
Nov 16, 2023
I love micro internal ultrasound for neck lipo. It liquifies the fat, making it easier and more safely remove the fat. The heat it creates also stimulates collagen in the skin and on top of the muscle which leads to skin and muscle tightening.
IV sedation vs. Local anesthesia for tumescent neck lipo?
Anesthesia type for neck lipo
Nov 16, 2023
Most of my patients have neck liposuction with local anesthesia. I give a little muscle shot for sedation so they are relaxed. If someone is very anxious or a control freak I recommend they have sedation or twilight anesthesia. They will not feel anything and just wake up when it is over.
Does lipo of neck and jawline always use microcannulas?
neck liposuction technique
Nov 16, 2023
In general the layer of fat in the neck is thin, so using a thinner cannula is best. I like to use internal ultrasound which breaks up the fat and tightens the skin. This is 1.8 mm thin. I then remove the fat with a 2.0 mm spatula cannula, which only has the suction on the underside so a thin layer of fat is left just under the skin.
Should I do chin lipo too or just filler?
small chin and neck fat
Nov 16, 2023
You definately have a small chin but it does not look like you have much neck fat. I would consider a chin implant for the most reliable projection. Any little bit of fat under the chin will not be seen as much.
In my 20s, what are my options for improving the appearance of my lower face?
Fat grafting to chin
Nov 16, 2023
Your chin looks a little small, deflated and asymmetric. Fat grating is a great, natural option to strengthen the jawline and it will also camoflague the little jowls you have, as well as, improve the symmetry.

Is it true that fat can migration down and cause sagging after facial fat transfer?
Fat graft to face and calves
Nov 2, 2023
No drift with fat grafting. It is permanent movement of fat cells, so if you gain or lose weight in the future the area will get bigger or smaller. I do not like fat graft to calves, implants are a more reliable and powerful solution.

breast implant revision, possible tubular breasts
