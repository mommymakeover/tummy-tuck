Do I need a crown on these root canal premolars?
Dental Crowns
Sep 28, 2018
After root canal therapy, the tooth no longer has a root and is dead. It is recommended to have crowns placed on RCT teeth for extra protection since it loses structure, and also for cosmetic purposes.

Bone loss

If you don’t have enough bone, the bone can be rebuilt. It may be possible to have a bone graft placed to create a better structure for a new implant. Why are my gums darkening around a porcelain crown?
Gums Darkening
Sep 14, 2018
Based on my experience, darkening around a porcelain crown is more likely to be present whenever metal is used as a substructure for the crown ( PFM or porcelain fused to metal / gold crowns). PFM crowns have an opaque “non-precious metal” , “semi-precious metal” or “high noble metal” alloy substructure that seals the tooth and offers support for the tooth colored ceramic that is stacked on top.
