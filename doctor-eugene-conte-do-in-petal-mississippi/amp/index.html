Can you remove birthmarks on the legs?
Removal of Leg Birthmarks
Apr 10, 2017
It depends on what kind of birthmarks you have. If they are pigmented birthmarks (nevi), it may be difficult to remove them without scarring. If they are vascular birthmarks, meaning the growth itself is made up of blood vessels then there are laser therapies that can be effective with minimal scarring. Numerous birth marks covering large areas can be a cause for concern, so make sure you have them evaluated by a board certified dermatologist

Isotroin While Pregnant

Hi there,
You must stop taking the medication immediately and notify your doctor.
