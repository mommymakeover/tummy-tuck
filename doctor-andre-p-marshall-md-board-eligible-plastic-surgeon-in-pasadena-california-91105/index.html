Can I get a BBL with a flank (lumbar) hernia?
BBL with hernia
Aug 28, 2022
Hi kcoss_,
If you have any underling anatomical abnormalities such as a lumbar hernia it is best to get this treated and resolved prior to liposuction and BBL. A hernia is an abnormal opening. During liposuction the instruments used to remove fat could enter into this opening and cause a life threatening injury. So it is important to have this repaired well in advance of any elective aesthetic surgery on that part of your body.
