Breast Aug 3 Months Ago - Areola Shape and Location
Post Breast Augmentation Areola Changes
Jun 5, 2011
It is possible for the Areola to be positioned too high following breast augmentation. This could be a result of your anatomy pre-operatively. If the lower half (or inferior pole) of breast skin stretches, the breast "bottoms out" resulting in a nipple areola complex in a higher position than desired.
