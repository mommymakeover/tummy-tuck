Can you perform microdermabrasion with peel same time?
Microdermabrasion with Chemical Peel
Nov 30, 2017
Yes, the two procedures can be combined. Depending on the experience of your skincare provider and preference, there is no wrong way to do the treatment. Some esthetician’s prefer to do the microdermabrasion first using the diamond tip hand piece (non aluminum oxide crystals) then either a Chemical Peel or a more milder Exfoliation or vice versa.
