Could botox injections be the problem?
Botox can cause Double Vision
Dec 6, 2013
Yes, Botox can cause double vision if the toxin spread to one or more of the 6 tiny muscles involved in the coordinated movements of your eyes. Please see an ophthalmologist or oculoplastic surgeon for a thorough evaluation, diagnosis and treatment. Botox effects can last between 3 and 6 months. Unintended effects often resolve more quickly. Good luck.
Is there any evidence that autoimmune disease can cause either Botox or fillers like Juederm, etc
Autoimmune Disorders and Botox
Dec 6, 2013
I am not aware of any proven relationship between autoimmune disorders and diminished duration of effect with Botox or similar neurotoxins. However, theoretically, people with auto-immune disorders may make antibodies to Botox more readily; thus, promoting a decreased duration of effect. May be worth trying a different brand, like Dysport or Xeomin.
Botox or Fillers for Under Eye Wrinkle?
Botox vs. Filler for Under Eye Wrinkle
Dec 6, 2013
We use both Botox and / or hyaluronic fillers to improve under eye wrinkles and / or bags. Also, chemical or laser peeling may be helpful alone or in combination with Botox or fillers. For the best advice, I recommend being evaluated in person by a board certified dermatologist, plastic surgeon or oculoplastic surgeon who specializes in these treatments.
Can botox cause acne?
Botox does not cause Acne
Dec 6, 2013
Botox does not cause acne to my knowledge nor in my ~ 20 years experience. Acne is a very common problem and most likely just coincindental.
