I want to have facial filler, but I'm going abroad in 2 weeks. What do you think?
Activity Post Filler
May 23, 2017
This is a great question. There is no restriction on activities two weeks after getting fillers so there is no reason not to travel. Please keep in mind that you may have some bruising and swelling after your injectable treatment that may last up to 7 days. To minimize these possible side effects be sure to discontinue fish oils, aspirin and any other blood thinning agents. Taking oral arnica pellets and applying arnica gel daily post treatment may help minimize any bruising. Safe travels!

Will a chemical peel worsen my post inflammatory hyperpigmentation?
Post Inflammatory Hyperpigmentation Treatment
May 23, 2017
This is a great question. The best approach is to start with a very conservative and mild approach to not irritate your skin. First off you should start using a daily broad spectrum sunscreen even if you are not outdoors all day. I would recommend starting with a very gentle peel (10% glycolic or less) to see how your skin reacts. If you skin tolerates the peel, then the next time do you a slightly stronger peel. Good luck!
