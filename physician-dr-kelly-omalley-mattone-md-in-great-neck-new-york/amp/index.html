Would I benefit from Botox for marionette lines?
Treatment options for mouth folds
Jul 25, 2023
Smile lines from your nose to your mouth, called nasolabial folds (NLF) are not treated with Botox. It’s best to treat the NLF with Ultherapy to lift the cheeks that are causing the folds to begin with. Another option is to inject cheek filler to temporarily lift the folds.
Will your forehead still be able to move after Botox injection?
How to get the best Botox results
Jul 25, 2023
My advice is to please be honest and discuss the look you are trying to achieve with your doctor/injector. We can control the amount of movement and meet your expectations, if made aware of them.
Botox to flip the bottom lip?
Botox lip flip for bottom lip
Jul 25, 2023
Yes, you can flip the bottom lip with Botox. It’s a quick and easy procedure. Personally, I use Jeaveau on mine.
Can you raise your eyebrows after treatment for 11 lines?
Botox for 11’s
Jul 25, 2023
The 11’s are caused by muscles that allow your brows to come together, as in a mad or frowning face. Treating them stops you from moving them towards each other. Raising your eyebrows uses different muscles than the ones treated in the 11’s. So you should have full use of the brows after treating the 11’s.
Can I eat after getting Botox around my mouth and lips?
Eating after Botox around mouth and lips
Jul 25, 2023
If you have been numbed either topically or with a block after mouth and lip injections, it’s best to wait until the numbing has completely worn off before you eat or you may bite your lips or tongue. Otherwise, it’s safe to eat after.
What filler is best for frown lines?
NO FILLER BETWEEN THE BROWS
Jul 25, 2023
It’s very risky to treat frown lines or wrinkles with filler. It’s a very vascular area and if filler gets into an artery it can cause a vascular occlusion or simply put, a blockage in the artery that supplies blood and oxygen to your eyes, which can result in blindness and tissue necrosis. Best to treat with Botox!!
Can filler be used to reduce the appearance of forehead lines?
Botox is best for forehead lines
Jul 25, 2023
It is very risky to treat forehead lines with filler. Especially between the brow. It’s a very vascular area and if filler gets into an artery it can cause a VO vascular occlusion, which can result in blindness and tissue necrosis. Best to treat with Botox.
