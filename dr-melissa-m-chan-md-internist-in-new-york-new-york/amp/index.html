How long should I avoid sun exposure after AviClear for optimal results?
AviClear Sun Exposure
Feb 27, 2023
AviClear is a laser treatment and with all laser treatments, sun exposure should be minimalized throughout the whole treatment. After any laser treatments, redness, swelling, and sensitivity may occur, prolonged sun exposure may cause pigmentation changes. If your job does not allow you to work away from long sun exposures, I would recommend a wide brim sun hat that is UPF 50+ with a strict regimen of application of mineral sunscreen SPF 30+ every 2 hours.
