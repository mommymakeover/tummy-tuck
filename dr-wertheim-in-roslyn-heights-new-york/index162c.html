What is the ideal age for Emface treatments?
emface ideal age?
Mar 6, 2023
There is no ideal age. In my practice, the average age is approx 50. That being said at any age when you start seeing wrinkles, loose skin, and loss of volume its time! There are many choosing to do emface "preventatively" or as soon as they become concerned with facial changes.. I have treated patients from 30 to 85!

rf will not effect facial fat
Mar 6, 2023
Yes, the emface can be performed without the rf. However the studies that have been done show that the strength of the facial RF is not sufficient to kill fat but enough to cause a tightening of the skin.
