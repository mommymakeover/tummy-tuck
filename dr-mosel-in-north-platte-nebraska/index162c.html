Recommended number of Kybella treatments?
When your happy, we are happy
Oct 26, 2016
The number of treatments to achieve optimal results can vary from 1-6 treatments, but the average in my experience is 2-3. If you are satisfied after two treatments then I would recommend stopping at that point. We recommend waiting at least 4-6 weeks between treatments, but there is no upper time limit and you could always decide to get a 3rd treatment at any point after that.
How long should I wait in between PDT sessions for acne? My nurse said 2-3 months, but is it safe to d
Treatment protocols may vary
Sep 26, 2016
Treatment of acne with PDT is considered "off-label" so there is no official guidelines on how often it may be done, however in my practice we try to perform the procedure every 4-6 weeks for the first few treatments, and then start to gradually space them apart further for maintenance.

Within 3-4 days you will start to see the initial effects, and then by 12-14 days you will have full benefit. There is no limit to the number of times you may receive Botox, and it's likely that consistent use will prevent future wrinkles from developing.
