Why are few plastic surgeons experienced in TUBA trans-umbilical breast aug? What am I missing?
TUBA Breast Augmentation
Jan 22, 2018
First of all a saline implant must be used. This is not as lifelike as a silicone gel implant. Any postoperative complications will usually require another additional incision and resultant scar. The technique is not as precise a procedure compared to the standard approaches. The advantage of an umbilical scar does not seem to outweigh the disadvantages for most Board Certified Plastic Surgeons.
Recovery time of breast augmentation: how long you think recovery would be?
Breast Augmentation Recovery time
Jan 22, 2018
Every surgeon is somewhat different as to their recommendations for their patients. Most patients can resume an active life style after 3 to 4 weeks, although there may still be some discomfort with some activities. A consultation with a Board Certified Plastic Surgeon will determine what is best for you.
