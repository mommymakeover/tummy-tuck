Will Emsculpt destroy the fat graft to my buttocks?
Emsculpt for Buttocks.
Apr 5, 2019
The answer is no. The setting for the buttocks is in a muscle building mode as apposed to the abdomen which includes muscle and fat. Patients usually note an elevation of the buttocks which would further enhance the contour of the buttocks.
