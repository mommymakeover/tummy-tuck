I have the sickle cell disease. Should I forget about wanting cosmetic surgery specifically a
BBL in SICKLE CELL DISEASE PATIENTS
Nov 15, 2022
Patients with sickle cell disease are at higher risks due to the underlying red blood cell abnormality. Your RBCs have a decreased oxygen carrying capacity and this can be problematic in the immediate post operative period as well as long term. In addition, because of the sickling of the cells, they can get trapped and lead to tissue ischemia.

Most surgeons would not recommend elective surgery in patients with sickle cell because of the underlying risks, which can also include death.
