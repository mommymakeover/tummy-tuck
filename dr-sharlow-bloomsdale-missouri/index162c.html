Can I have SculpSure done to abdomen area if still breastfeeding baby who is one and gettin
Breastfeeding and Sculpture
Oct 16, 2019
Sculpture is a safe hyperthermic treatment for unwanted fat cells. This is a localized treatment and is considered very safe while breast-feeding. Over time, the body naturally illuminates these fat cells that have been damaged, and there are no risks to your baby.
SculpSure/truSculpting and compromised lymph system: can lipo damage the lymph nodes?
Lymph nodes damaged during liposuction…
Sep 20, 2019
Lymphatic channels are interrupted by liposuction of the abdomen, but lymph nodes are not. These channels regenerate with time, usually a few weeks, and if done by a qualified physician, you should not have any concerns. I would recommend that you proceed with your Sculpture procedure and follow the recommendations given by your provider of this procedure. You body will do the rest.
Am I a good candidate for smart lipo on the abdomen? I am 35 and have 2 children.
Am I a candidate for liposuction…
Sep 20, 2019
You do have a significant amount of stretch marks that, unfortunately, will interfere with the tightening effect that can be gain with the laser used during smartlipo. If you are interested in removal of fat and skin tightening, you would be better served with a tummy tuck procedure. Smartlipo shows very good to excellent results when there is excess fat and skin laxity, but this does require a healthy dermis. Finding a well qualified plastic surgeon with experience would be your best option…
